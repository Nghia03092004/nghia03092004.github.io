\section{Problem}
Count Gaussian primes $a+bi$ with $0 \le a,b \le 500$.
\begin{theorem}[Gaussian Prime Classification]
$a+bi$ is Gaussian prime iff: (i) $a,b>0$ and $a^2+b^2$ is rational prime, or (ii) one is 0 and the other is prime $\equiv 3 \pmod{4}$.
\end{theorem}
