\section{Problem Statement}
Wrap $n$ unit cubes together vs separately. $g(n)$ = max paper savings. $G(N)=\sum g(n)$. Given $G(18)=530, G(10^6)\equiv 951640919 \pmod{10^9+7}$. Find $G(10^{{16}}) \bmod 10^9+7$.

\section{Analysis}
$g(n)$ = $6n - S(n)$ where $S(n)$ is the surface area of the optimal rectangular box containing $n$ unit cubes. $S(n) = \min_{{abc\ge n}} 2(ab+bc+ca)$ where $a,b,c$ are positive integers. The sum $G(N)$ involves summing over all $n$ the minimum-surface-area box.
