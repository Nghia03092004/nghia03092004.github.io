\section{Problem}
Envy-free cake division among $n$ players.
\begin{theorem}[Stromquist]
An envy-free division always exists for continuous valuations.
\end{theorem}
\begin{theorem}[Selfridge--Conway]
Three-player envy-free division needs at most 5 cuts.
\end{theorem}
