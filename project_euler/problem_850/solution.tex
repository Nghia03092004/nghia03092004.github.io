\section{Problem Statement}
Count Conway--Coxeter frieze patterns of order~$n$.
\begin{theorem}[Conway--Coxeter]
$F(n) = C_{n-1}$ where $C_k = \frac{1}{k+1}\binom{2k}{k}$ is the Catalan number.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}[Proof sketch]
Bijection with triangulations of the $(n+1)$-gon. The unimodular rule $ad - bc = 1$ encodes triangle adjacency.
\end{proof}
