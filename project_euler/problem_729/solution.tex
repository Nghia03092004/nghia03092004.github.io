\section{Problem Statement}
Consider the recurrence
\[
a_{n+1}=a_n-\frac{1}{a_n}.
\]
Let $S(P)$ be the sum of the ranges of all periodic orbits with period at most $P$. The problem asks for $S(25)$ to four decimal places.
