\section{Problem Statement}

For $x > y$, generate a sequence $a_n$ by $a_x=y$ and $a_{n+1}=a_n^2 \bmod n$. Let $l(x,y)$ be the length until 0, $g(x)=\max l(x,y)$ for $y<x$, and $f(n)=\max g(x)$ for $x\le n$. Given $f(100)=145$, find $f(3000000)$.

\section{Mathematical Analysis}

Quadratic iteration mod n. Cycle detection via Floyd/Brent. Maximum orbit length.

\section{Complexity Analysis}

A direct search over all $x \le N$ runs in $O(N\sqrt{N})$.
