\begin{theorem}
$|GL(n, p)| = \prod_{k=0}^{n-1}(p^n - p^k)$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Row $k+1$ must avoid the $k$-dimensional span of previous rows: $p^n - p^k$ choices.
\end{proof}
For $n=10$, $p=2$: $|GL(10,2)| \bmod (10^9+7) = 731930254$.
