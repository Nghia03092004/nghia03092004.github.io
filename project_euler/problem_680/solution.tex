\section{Problem Statement}

Let $A[0..N-1]$ satisfy $A[i]=i$ initially. Perform $K$ reversal operations. Define $R(N,K)=\sum iA[i]$ after the reversals. Find $R(10^{18}, 10^6)\bmod 10^9$.

\section{Mathematical Analysis}

Balanced BST / treap for reversals, or algebraic analysis of reversal permutations

\section{Complexity Analysis}

A naive method is $O(KN)$, while a treap-based method is $O(K \log N)$.
