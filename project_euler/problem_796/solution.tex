\section{Problem Statement}
Ten decks of 54 cards each are shuffled together, for a total of 540 cards. Cards are drawn without replacement, and the goal is to reach a state where every suit, every rank, and every deck design has appeared at least once. The problem asks for the expected number of draws, rounded to eight decimal places.
