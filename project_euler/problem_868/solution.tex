\section{Problem}
Count words of length $n$ over $\Sigma_k$ avoiding 4th powers $xxxx$.
\begin{theorem}
$q(n,k) \sim C_k \alpha_k^n$ with $\alpha_2 \approx 1.8$.
\end{theorem}
\section{Method}
Backtracking with suffix check, or transfer matrix on DFA states.
