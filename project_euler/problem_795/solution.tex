\section{Problem Statement}
Define
\[
g(n)=\sum_{i=1}^{n} (-1)^i \gcd(n,i^2),
\qquad
G(N)=\sum_{n=1}^{N} g(n).
\]
Given $g(1234)=1233$ and $G(1234)=2194708$, determine $G(12345678)$.
