The sequence \(a_n\) is defined by \(a_1=1\), and then recursively for \(n\geq 1\): \begin {align*} a_{2n} &= 2a_n\\ a_{2n+1} &= a_n-3a_{n+1} \end {align*}

Define \(\displaystyle S(N) = \sum _{n=1}^N a_n\). You are given \(S(10) = -13\).
